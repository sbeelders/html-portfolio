Shannon's favourite movies

My top 5 favourite movies

  1. White Chicks

    2. This comedy film follows FBI agents Kevin and Marcus Copeland, who go undercover as two wealthy socialites after a failed kidnapping attempt leaves the actual heiresses comatose. Disguised in elaborate makeup and fashion, they must navigate high society, uncover the plot, and prevent the crime while maintaining their cover.

  2. The Other Woman

    3. This comedy centers on Carly, a successful lawyer who discovers that her boyfriend is married. When she meets his wife, Kate, they bond over their shared betrayal. Their dynamic shifts when they find out about a third woman, Amber. Together, the three women plot to exact revenge on the cheating man, leading to humorous and unexpected situations.

  3. The Princess Diaries

    4. This family comedy follows Mia Thermopolis, a teenage girl who discovers she is the heir to the throne of the fictional European country of Genovia. With the help of her estranged grandmother, Queen Clarisse, Mia undergoes a transformation to become a proper princess, while navigating the challenges of adolescence and royal responsibilities.
